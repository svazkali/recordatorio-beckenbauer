Franz Anton Beckenbauer

'Der Kaiser', leyenda del fútbol alemán. Capitán y campeón en 1974, pionero del libero, embajador, único en ganar como jugador y entrenador en la Copa del Mundo

About the Legend

☛Franz Beckenbauer, nicknamed 'Der Kaiser,' was born on September 11, 1945, in Munich, Germany.

☛ He served as the captain, leading Germany to victory in the 1974 FIFA World Cup and the Euro 1972.

☛ Beckenbauer, a versatile player, excelled in Bayern Munich, contributing to numerous league and cup triumphs.

☛ Pioneering the libero position, he revolutionized defensive tactics, showcasing tactical brilliance.

☛ In 1990, Beckenbauer achieved a historic feat by coaching Germany to another World Cup triumph.

☛ His career spanned from Bayern Munich to the New York Cosmos, influencing football globally.

☛ Inducted into various football halls of fame, Beckenbauer's legacy endures, symbolizing an era of triumph and innovation.

☛ As president of Bayern Munich, he continued shaping the club's success, leaving an indelible mark.

☛ Beckenbauer's impact extended globally as a football ambassador, contributing to the sport's growth.
